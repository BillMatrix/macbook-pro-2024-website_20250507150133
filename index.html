Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in the MacBook Pro 2024?

The MacBook Pro 2024 features the new M3 Pro and M3 Max chips, improved GPU performance, longer battery life, enhanced cooling system, and support for up to 128GB of unified memory.

How long is the battery life?

The 16-inch MacBook Pro 2024 offers up to 22 hours of battery life, while the 14-inch model provides up to 18 hours, making them the longest-lasting MacBooks ever.

Does it support external displays?

Yes, the MacBook Pro 2024 with M3 Pro can support up to two external displays with up to 6K resolution, while the M3 Max models can support up to four external displays.

Is it good for gaming?

The MacBook Pro 2024 offers excellent gaming performance thanks to its powerful M3 Pro and M3 Max chips with up to 40-core GPU. It can handle AAA games with high frame rates and supports hardware-accelerated ray tracing.
